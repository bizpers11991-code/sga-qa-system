# SGA QA Pack - User Manual

## 1. Foreman's Guide

### Logging In
Access the application URL provided by your administrator. Use your company credentials to log in via the "Login / Get Started" button.

### Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard is your main hub. It shows:
-   **Today's Focus:** Highlights reports that are overdue or due today.
-   **Pending Submissions:** Reports saved locally on your device because you were offline. They will submit automatically when you have an internet connection.
-   **Open Jobs:** A list of all your assigned jobs that need a QA Pack completed.
-   **My Submitted QA Packs:** A history of reports you have already submitted.
-   **My Incident Reports:** A history of any incidents, near misses, or hazards you have reported.

### Completing a QA Pack
1.  **Select a Job:** Tap on a job from the "Open Jobs" list to begin.
2.  **Navigate Forms:** The top of the screen has tabs for each required form (Job Sheet, Daily Report, ITP, etc.).
3.  **Guided Mode:** For a step-by-step experience, enable "Guided Mode" using the toggle switch. This will walk you through each form one by one.
4.  **Auto-Save:** Your progress is saved automatically as a draft every few seconds. You'll see a "Saving..." and "Saved" indicator in the header.
5.  **Offline Mode:** If you lose your internet connection, the app will continue to function. Your draft will be saved locally on your device. The status indicator will show "Offline".

### Submitting a Report
1.  Once all forms are complete, tap **"Review & Submit QA Pack"**.
2.  A **Biometric Verification** window will appear. This is your final sign-off.
3.  **Capture Photo:** Tap "Capture Photo" to take a real-time photo of yourself.
4.  **Provide Signature:** Use your finger or a stylus to sign in the signature box.
5.  **Confirm & Submit:** Tap "Confirm & Submit QA Pack". The app will prepare the final PDF and upload all data. Keep the window open until you see "Submission complete!".

### Reporting an Incident
1.  From the dashboard, tap the **"Report New Incident"** button.
2.  Fill out the form with details about the incident, near miss, or hazard.
3.  You can attach photos for evidence.
4.  Tap **"Submit Report"**. This will be sent directly to the HSEQ manager.

## 2. Administrator's Guide

The Admin Dashboard is a powerful tool for managing jobs, reviewing reports, and ensuring compliance.

### Navigating the Dashboard
Use the sidebar on the left to navigate between different views:
-   **Summary:** An overview of reports needing review, overdue jobs, and other key metrics.
-   **Scheduler:** A calendar-based view of all jobs assigned to foremen.
-   **Create Job:** The main hub for creating new jobs.
-   **Report Archive:** A searchable and filterable list of all submitted QA packs.
-   **Incidents / NCR Register:** View and manage all incident and non-conformance reports.
-   **Analytics:** View high-level dashboards on divisional performance.
-   **Resources:** Manage the master list of crew members and equipment.
-   **Document Library:** Upload and manage company-wide documents like specifications and procedures.

### Creating a New Job
1.  Navigate to **"Create Job"**.
2.  **AI Extraction (Recommended):** Upload a PDF or image of the job sheet. The AI will automatically extract the details. A job editor window will pop up with the pre-filled information.
3.  **Manual Creation:** Use the "Create Job Manually" form for quick entry, or the "Advanced Editor" for full control over the job sheet details.
4.  **Review and Assign:** In the editor, review all details, assign a foreman, and set the job and due dates.
5.  **Save Job:** Click "Save Job". The job will be created, and a notification with the PDF job sheet will be sent to the appropriate Teams channel.

### Reviewing a QA Pack
1.  From the **Summary** view or **Report Archive**, click on a report to open the **Details Modal**.
2.  **Review Details:** The modal shows the AI-generated executive summary and all report details. You can switch between different versions of the report using the history sidebar.
3.  **Update Status:** Use the "Review Panel" on the right to update the report's status (e.g., to "Reviewed" or "Approved") and add internal notes.
4.  **Edit Report:** If changes are needed, click the "Edit" button to open the full QA Pack editor. You can make changes and re-submit, which will create a new version of the report.
5.  **Raise NCR:** If a non-conformance is identified, click "Raise NCR" to be taken directly to the NCR creation form with the job number pre-filled.
